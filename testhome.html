About Jess Hollenbaugh

Jess is an undergraduate student in the Honors College at Portland State Univesity. A physics major with mathematics and music minors, Jess is passionate about and dedicated to both the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) and the power of music to make people human. She is a violist in the PSU orchestra as well as a chamber musician and private instructor on both viola and violin.

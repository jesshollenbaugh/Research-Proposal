Method and Design

First I would take a random sample of STEM majors and do a simple survey to see what proportion have had formal music education, past or present. The proportion may vary depending on location, so it would be prudent to either take a large and diverse sample from many different universities or isolate it to one test site, i.e. Portland State University. An open ended survey with multiple questions would be most appropriate--have they received any formal music education? How many years? Is it continuing to this day?
